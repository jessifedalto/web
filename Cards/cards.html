Mechanics

Mechanics is the branch of physics responsible for studying movement. There is Classical, Relativistic and Quantum Mechanics. Mechanics is the branch of physics responsible for studying the movements of bodies, as well as their temporal evolution and the mathematical equations that determine them.

IoT

The Internet of Things (IoT) refers to the process of connecting everyday physical objects to the Internet to exchange data: light bulbs, medical devices, smart devices and even smart cities are all part of the Internet of Things.

Electronics

Electronics is the science that studies how to control electrical energy by electrical means in which electrons play a fundamental role. It is divided into analog and digital because its working coordinates choose to obey these two ways of presenting the electrical signals to be processed.
